Cyber security

Personal cybersecurity is crucial in today's digital landscape, where our lives are increasingly dependant on the technology we use. With sensitive information—such as bank details, personal messages, and health records—stored online, the risk of identity theft, financial loss, and privacy breaches is ever-present. Maintaining good cyber security practices will protect personal data and keep you safer online. By prioritizing cybersecurity, we can safeguard our digital identities and navigate the online world with greater confidence and peace of mind.
